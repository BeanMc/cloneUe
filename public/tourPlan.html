Day 1/23. Nov

Ulaanbaatar

On arrival in Ulaanbaatar, you will be greeted by your guide and driver. Transfer to the hotel. After some relaxing time take city tour including Gandan monastery, Sukhbaatar square, National History museum and Bogd Khaan Winter Palace museum. Dinner and overnight in the hotel. (L/D)

Day 2/24. Nov

Ulgii- Sagsai

Early morning flight to Ulgii. After arrival you will be greeted by the driver drive to Sagsai, where the Eagle hunter live. You will stay with the Kazakh family and have opportunity to photograph daily life and eagle hunters. (B/L/D)

Day 3/25. Nov

Sagsai - Ulgii

You will take photo for whole day. In the evening drive back to Ulgii. Have dinner and stay in the local hotel. (B/L/D)

Day 4/26. Nov

UB- Hustai National Park

In the morning time flight back to UB. After arrival drive directly to Hustai National park. Upon arrival view a short documentary and visit the museum. Drive deep into the National Park in search of Przewalski horses (takhi), ancestor of all modern-day horses in the world. If you have binoculars, please bring them. Overnight stay in a ger camp. (B/L/D)

Day 5/27. Nov

Karakorum, Erdene-Zuu Monastery

After breakfast, drive to Karakorum, once the capital of the 13th century Great Mongol Empire. Visit Erdene-Zuu Monastery, which was the first and the largest monastery in Mongolia built on the ruins of the ancient capital. Tour the monastery and visit several temples with excellent examples of Buddhist Thangka paintings and statues dating back to the 17th century. Visit the Karakorum museum. Dinner and overnight stay in a guest house. (B/L/D)

Day 6/28. Nov

Bayangobi

After breakfast, drive to Bayangobi. On this day visit a nomad family to photograph daily life of horse and camel breeder family. Dinner and overnight stay in the ger of the nomad family. (B/L/D)

Day 7/29. Nov

Ulaanbaatar

After early breakfast drive back to Ulaanbaatar. After arrival in the city transfer to the hotel and lunch. Have some shopping time for souvenir and cashmere. Dinner and overnight at the hotel. (B/L/D)

Day 8/30. Nov

Departure

You will picked up and transport to the airport for departure from Mongolia. (B)
